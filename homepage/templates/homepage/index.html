About

Employee Transport Solution For Enterprises

The idea of this project is to design and develop a smart cloud-based transportation solution for large enterprises to optimize the cost and the travel time of their transporting units along with maintaining the employee satisfaction. The system should be capable of utilizing the resources efficiently such that maximum objectives can be achieved with a minimal investment of time, money and manpower. This project is a ones stop solution for all the transport related services that an organization has to offer to its employees
